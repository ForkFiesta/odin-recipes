Hamburger Recipe

A Hamburger Cooked to Perfection

This "Best Hamburger Ever" recipe delivers a juicy and flavorful burger perfect for grilling. The patties are made from lean ground beef mixed with onion, Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese, egg, and a flavorful blend of seasonings, including onion soup mix, garlic, soy sauce, and herbs like parsley and rosemary. Grilled to perfection in about 4-5 minutes per side, these burgers are easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings. Serve them with classic sides like potato wedges or pasta salad for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dired oregano
  • 1/2 tsp crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix lean ground beef with chopped onion, grated cheese, egg, and seasonings like onion soup mix, garlic, soy sauce, parsley, and rosemary.
  2. Form the mixture into patties.
  3. Pregeat the grill to medium-high heat
  4. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side until fully cooked
  5. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings
